You see, a lot of people around me are indecisive, and because of it they have a high anxiety level. With any little thing they get a panic attack and shut down for few days. Sometimes it is just impossible to get people decide on a restaurant to go for lunch, then of course we, as a society, are going to stumble when it comes to bigger decisions. Commitment seems to be a great issue for a lot of us. Whether it is commitment to a person or simply a commitment to daily routines.


This is a classic example of a group of people who can't come to an agreement on what to do. A leader must step in during these situations. Yes, a decisive and adventurous person must set everyone free of their imaginary boundaries and limitations.
